Welcome

written by Nycticorax on 2020-12-20

Welcome to "An Adventurous Journey Between Shower And Coffee". This website is mostly a host for my blog, which has two purposes:

  1. Help me structure of my thoughts. I suffer from having concepts, ideas and train of thoughts interspersed with noise and things that actually belong to the trash bin. I intend the blog to help me sort them out and structure my thoughts.
  2. Hopefully put out in the open useful things. As someone once said, from a cosmic point of view, threading ideas, no matter how good, is just chair-warming. It's sharing that makes the difference. So let's try to make a humble and friendly difference.
